4. Principles

Transparency

Open source software promotes trust from users and crowdsources the development effort to a willing constituency.

Community

Giving end-users more control encourages their level of participation. Continued development of Swagger and other open-source projects earns us respect in the developer community.

Agility

We are a small passionate team with all the necessary skills, free from the constraints of hierarchy, creative mediocrity, disorder, and dependency.

Passion

We love the work we do. We dream about it, talk to our families about it, and devote our weekends to it.
